About COVID-19

COVID-19 is a virus that has been spread all around the globe and has become a global pandemic.

When it started?

COVID-19 formerly referred as 'novel coronavirus' was first reported from Wuhan, China on 31st December 2019.

When it entered India?

The very first case of COVID-19 was reported from Thrissur, Kerala on 30th January 2020.

What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

The common symptoms of COVID-19 includes normal to high fever, body pain, restlessness, breathing problems, joint pain.
